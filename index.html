About API BeerCo

At API BeerCo, we brew beer with friends and openly share everything—recipes, techniques, and experiences—embracing an open-source mindset. Brewing for us is a fusion of creativity, science, and community. Each batch is a hands-on learning experience where we experiment, refine, and iterate, benefiting from the fast feedback loop of beer’s short fermentation cycle. We love building things, and brewing is another way to channel that passion. Plus, tasting the by-product isn’t too bad either! :)
